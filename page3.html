About:Step-By-Step Procedure

Procedure to insert data

  • Step1:Go into google and enter the refrence link for the website.
  • Step2:Go through each divisions to know more about the website and press the hyper-link available in the manhole-services option to redirect you to page to insert data.
  • Step3:In the given coloumns fill the respective data's and hit "OK" button to insert data into table.
    • Select the location tab from the table which will redirect you to google map interface.
    • Choose your location from the map.
    • The data will automatically be entered into the coloumn.
  • Step4:After cofirming the data's,Press "Confirm" button to store data into database.

Procedure to access data

  • Step1:To access data follow Step 1-2 to under insert data procedure.
  • Step2:Go to find data section and follow section 1-2 of step3 from insert data procedure.
  • Step3:Hit confirm and the data's will be fetched from the database and displayed.
